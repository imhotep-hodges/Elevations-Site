Mission Statement

Elevations Beauty Bar and Suites intends to offer beauty professionals the opportunity to start and maintain their businesses independently yet together; empowering each other and providing new talent an affordable head start.


Philosophy and Vision

Elevations Beauty Bar and Suites is committed to creating a thriving and innovative community environment that highlights beauty artistry professionals. We strive to be the hot spot for our beauty professionals to keep abreast of the latest trends and introduce new ones by offering professional training and on-the-spot classes.

The Owner

Evelyn Murray

I am currently the proud owner of Elevations Beauty Bar in Hagerstown, Maryland. As a stylist, I have been in the beauty industry for over 15 years and have owned Elevations Beauty Bar for 2 years. Beauty has been a passion of mine since I was a small child, and I chose to start my career early as a shampoo girl for one of the busiest salons in town. I was able to thrive, gain hands-on training, feed my passion, and plot my future in the industry.

I am well versed in all textures of hair and enjoy giving the best possible experience to anyone sitting in my chair or in my Salon. My philosophy is: When you Look Good, you Feel Good.

I am committed to lifelong learning and keep up with the latest styles and trends in order to stay engaged, current, and to nurture new clients of all age groups.

Fatima's Natural Hair

Fatima Bey

I am a licensed Cosmetologist with 2 years of experience. My focus is on enhancing self-image through the care of natural hair. I provide information on healthy routines, low maintenance styles, scalp treatments, and recommend the best products for daily maintenance of all hair textures - curly, extra curly, straight, or wavy. I welcome children and anyone with natural or chemically treated hair.

Services offered include silk press, customized scalp treatments, two-strand twists, flat twists, loc maintenance, loc retwist and styles, basic cornrow, cuts, relaxer, and color.

In addition to my work as a Cosmetologist, I am a full-time Project Support Specialist for BETAH Associates.

In my personal life, I am a mother of four. I enjoy spending time with family, listening to music, traveling, appreciating the beauty of nature, staying active, and watching shows/movies of various genres such as horror, comedy, adventure, and drama.

Viniyah's Styles

Viniyah Parnell

Hi, I’m Viniyah Parnell, a dedicated hair braider based in the heart of Maryland. With over 10 years of experience, I’ve transformed my passion for hair into an art form. Born and raised in the charming town of Hagerstown, Maryland, my roots inspire my creativity.

From intricate braids to stylish twists, I’ve honed my skills to not just create hairstyles but to craft individual expressions of beauty. Beyond the salon chair, I’m fueled by the joy of connecting with people. Whether it’s sharing stories or creating a customized look that reflects your personality, my goal is to leave you not just satisfied but genuinely happy.

Being a part of the Maryland community is something I cherish. It’s a place where diversity meets unity, and I’m proud to contribute to the vibrant tapestry of styles. Let’s embark on a journey of self-expression and beauty together. Looking forward to bringing your vision to life, one braid at a time!
